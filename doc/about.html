Afterlife -- a map with elements of survival and HvH (human-vs-human) games. Each two turns, your units are copied to enemy part of the map to be controlled by AI. These copies get stronger every turn. Note: this map has similarities with ''Sacrifice survival'', but I hope it is more balanced.
